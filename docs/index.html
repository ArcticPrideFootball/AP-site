ABOUT US

2025 WESTERN CONFERENCE CHAMPIONS

Arctic Pride is Edmonton's WWCFL tackle football team. We are Edmonton's tackle football team in the Western Women's Canadian Football League. In our inaugural season, 2025, we became the Western Conference champions and hosted the WWCFL Championship at Commonwealth Stadium. We hope to return to the WWCFL championship again in our 2026 season!
